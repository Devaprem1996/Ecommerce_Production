# Domain Entity

Product

Definition

An item available for purchase.

Owns

Name

Price

Description

Images

Category

Brand

SKU

Inventory Reference

Business Rules

Must belong to one category.

Can appear in many carts.

Can appear in many orders.

Can receive many reviews.

---

# Domain Entity

Category

Definition

Logical grouping of products.

Business Rules

One category

↓

Many products

Categories may be hidden.

Deleting category never deletes products.

---

# Domain Entity

Inventory

Definition

Tracks product availability.

Business Rules

Inventory owns stock.

Products never own stock.

Stock cannot become negative.

Reserved stock cannot exceed available stock.

---

# Domain Entity

Cart

Definition

Temporary shopping basket.

Business Rules

Belongs to one customer.

Contains many products.

Dynamically queries the live product price; does not lock price until order creation.

Automatically recalculates totals.

---

# Domain Entity

Checkout

Definition

Temporary purchase process.

Business Rules

Cannot begin with empty cart.

Requires shipping address.

Requires payment method.

Creates order only after successful payment verification.

---

# Domain Entity

Payment

Definition

Financial transaction.

Business Rules

One payment belongs to one order.

Payment cannot exist without an order.

Payment status determines order creation.

Payment must be verified.

---

# Domain Entity

Order

Definition

Completed purchase.

Business Rules

Order belongs to one customer.

Contains many products.

Cannot be modified after confirmation.

Order history never changes.

---

# Domain Entity

Order Item

Business Rules

Stores

Name

Price

Quantity

Tax

Discount

---

# Domain Entity

Review

Definition

Customer feedback.

Business Rules

Only verified buyers may review.

Rating

1–5

Customer may edit own review.

Admin may hide inappropriate reviews.

---

# Domain Entity

Wishlist

Definition

Saved products.

Business Rules

Duplicate products not allowed.

Does not affect inventory.

Does not reserve stock.
